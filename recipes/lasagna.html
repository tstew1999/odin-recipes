Lasagna

In order to make this delicious dish you will need...

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Adult supervision
  • Love
  • A working oven
  • A meat of your choice
  • Cheese
  • A pan
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees then oil your pan of choice
  2. Place your first layer of pasta (Lasagne) on the pan and lather it in sauce
  3. Add some meat, maybe some cheese
  4. Lovingly add the salt and pepper
  5. Add some more sauce
  6. Repeat step 2 to 5 until you are satisfied
  7. Get a responsible adult to place your creation into the oven
  8. Leave it in there for a couple of minutes and boom
